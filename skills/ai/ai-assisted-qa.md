# AI-Assisted QA

## Overview

AI-assisted QA involves using language models and automation tools to enhance testing processes, improve efficiency, and support decision-making.

---

## Key Applications

### Test Case Generation

* Generate test cases from requirements
* Suggest edge cases and negative scenarios

---

### Bug Analysis

* Identify potential root causes
* Suggest fixes and improvements

---

### Requirement Validation

* Detect ambiguities
* Identify missing conditions

---

## Benefits

* Faster test design
* Improved coverage
* Reduced manual effort

---

## Limitations

* Requires validation by QA engineers
* May produce incomplete or incorrect outputs
* Depends on input quality

---

## Best Practices

* Use AI as a support tool, not a replacement
* Validate all generated outputs
* Combine with domain knowledge

---

## Summary

AI enhances QA processes by supporting analysis and test design, but human validation remains essential to ensure quality and accuracy.
